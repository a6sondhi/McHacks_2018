Immerse Welcome to the Immerse Web Application. Over the past 3 decades, the percentage Americans adults who read literature has dropped about 14%. The issue we discovered is that due to the rise of modern technologies, movies and other films are more captivating than reading a boring book. That is way we came up with Immerse. By utilizing IBM Watson and the Spotify's API, Immerse reads in text, analyzes the emotion of the text, and play music accordingly to the atmosphere. Text is brought to life and readers can feel more immersed into their novels. Users can either scan text through their mobile devices or simply input the text through this web app.
